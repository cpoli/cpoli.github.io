Tight-binding models


TB is a package providing a Python implementation, with heavy use of numpy vectorization, of Tight-Binding models. It can be used to build up and solve tight-binding models with complex-valued onsite energies and hoppings.

               

Tight-binding chain (with TB module)


with a special emphasis on PT-symmetry and protected zero-modes.

               

Tight-binding Lieb lattice (with TB module)


with a special emphasis on protected zero-modes induced by reduced chiral symmetry.

               

Tight-binding Graphene (new version using TB module coming soon)


with a special emphasis on pseudo Landau levels and localized states induced by vacancy defects and negative couplings.
