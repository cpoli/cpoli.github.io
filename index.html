 About Me


I'm a theoretical physicist with experience in analyzing complex systems, such as chaotic quantum systems, topologically protected states, quantum-orbit analysis, and strained graphene, using analytical models and personally built numerical simulations.


Throughout my career I have built numerous codes and analytical models using MATLAB and C++, but once I discovered Python I never looked back. Having developed a fascination for Big Data, Data Science, and Machine Learning, I now want to apply the skills and knowledge acquired within the past nine years (that’s how long I’ve been in Academia!), to build a career as a Python Developer.

In any case, I hope you will enjoy surfing through my website just as much as I enjoyed building it!
