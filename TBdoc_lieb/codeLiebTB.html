liebTB code¶

liebTB module models a line centered squared lattice (Lieb lattice) with real and/or complex valued hoppings and onsite energies.

liebTB is available at https://github.com/cpoli/TB

set_disorder_uniform(alpha)[source]¶

Set a non generic disorder. Disorder uniform along math:y for math:t_{ab} and math:t_{ba}, and uniform along math:x for math:t_{ac} and math:t_{ca}.

Parameters:alpha – Stength of the disorder.

Note

This disorder preserves the zero mode.
